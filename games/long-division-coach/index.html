Long division is a repeating cycle. Your job is to do one tiny step at a time.

  1. Divide: How many of the divisor fit in the current number? (For example: “How many 5s are in 20?”)
  2. Multiply: Multiply the divisor by that number.
  3. Subtract: Subtract to see what’s left.
  4. Bring down: Bring down the next digit (if there is one).
Tip

If the first digit is too small, you can “grab one more digit” from the dividend.
