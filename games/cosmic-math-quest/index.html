How to play

  • Pick Easy, Medium, or Hard. Tap Show hint for help (pictures on Easy).
  • Choose Multiplication, Division, or Mix.
  • Type your answer and press Check or Enter. Then press Next question when you’re ready.
  • Each correct answer is +10 points. Every 3 in a row adds +5 bonus!
  • No timer — finish all 10 questions. Hearts are lives, and if they run out the mission pauses so you can try again or fix tricky questions.
